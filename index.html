About

The Artist

Neoclassical guitar is where centuries of classical tradition meet the unbounded energy of modern music. It is a genre built on precision, emotion, and the relentless pursuit of beauty — and it is at the heart of everything Dan plays.

Dan's journey began with the great classical composers — Bach, Paganini, Vivaldi — and expanded outward into the electric landscape of contemporary guitar. Drawing on fingerstyle technique, counterpoint, and harmonic complexity, his music bridges two worlds with fluency and intention.

Whether performing sweeping arpeggiated passages reminiscent of a baroque concerto or driving riffs that pulse with modern intensity, Dan brings a singular voice to every note. His compositions are architectural — layered, deliberate, and expressive in ways that go beyond genre.

Based on the East Coast, Dan is currently developing his debut recordings and expanding his live performance catalogue. This portfolio is the beginning of something larger.
